# Leave of Absence Policy

## Sick Leave

- **US**: Up to 10 days paid sick leave per year. FMLA applies for serious health conditions (up to 12 weeks unpaid, job-protected).
- **UK**: Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) from day 4 of illness. Company tops up to full pay for up to 10 days per year.
- **Germany**: Full pay for up to 6 weeks per illness episode, covered by statutory health insurance thereafter.

## Parental Leave

- **US**: 12 weeks paid parental leave for primary caregivers; 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.
- **UK**: Statutory Maternity/Paternity Leave applies. Company tops up to full pay for the first 16 weeks for primary caregivers.
- **Germany**: Parental leave (Elternzeit) up to 3 years per child; company pays full salary for the first 14 weeks.

## Bereavement Leave

- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent): 5 paid days.
- Extended family (grandparent, sibling, in-law): 3 paid days.
- Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager discretion.

## Emergency / Unplanned Leave

Notify your manager and HR as soon as possible. Documentation may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Requesting Leave

Submit a formal leave request via the HR portal. Longer leaves (>2 weeks) require HR approval in addition to manager sign-off.
