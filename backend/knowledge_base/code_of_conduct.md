# Code of Conduct & Workplace Policy

## Core Principles

All employees are expected to act with integrity, respect, and professionalism. We are committed to a workplace free from harassment, discrimination, and retaliation.

## Prohibited Conduct

- Harassment of any kind - verbal, physical, or visual - based on any protected characteristic (gender, race, nationality, age, disability, religion, sexual orientation, etc.).
- Discrimination in hiring, promotions, compensation, or any employment decision.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.
- Bullying, intimidation, or hostile behavior toward colleagues, managers, or direct reports.

## Reporting

Employees who experience or witness a violation should:
1. Report to their HR Business Partner directly.
2. Use the anonymous Ethics Hotline (available 24/7 on the intranet).
3. Contact the Legal/Compliance team for potential legal violations.

All reports are treated confidentially to the extent possible. HR will investigate promptly and impartially.

## Conflict of Interest

Employees must disclose any outside business interests or personal relationships that may conflict with company interests. Disclosure forms are available in the HR portal.

## Disciplinary Process

Violations may result in coaching, written warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity. Serious violations (e.g., harassment, fraud) may result in immediate termination pending investigation.

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers are expected to model company values, address inappropriate behavior promptly, and escalate concerns to HR without delay.
