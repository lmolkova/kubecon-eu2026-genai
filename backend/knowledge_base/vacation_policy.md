# Vacation Policy

## Accrual

- **US employees**: 20 days PTO per year, accrued monthly (1.67 days/month).
- **UK employees**: 28 days statutory minimum; company provides 28 days.
- **Germany employees**: 30 days statutory minimum; company provides 30 days.

## Carryover

- US: Up to 5 unused days may carry over to the next calendar year. All remaining days are forfeited on January 31.
- UK: Up to 5 days may be carried over by mutual agreement with the manager.
- Germany: All unused days must be taken by March 31 of the following year.

## Requesting Time Off

1. Submit a request in the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for absences up to 1 week.
2. Requests of more than 1 week require 2 weeks advance notice and manager approval.
3. Peak-period blackouts (year-end, major product launches) may limit approvals — check with your manager.

## Vacation Balance

Employees can view their current balance in the HR portal. The balance reflects days accrued minus days used and any approved upcoming leave.
