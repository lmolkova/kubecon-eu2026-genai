# Compensation Policy

## Salary Review Cycle

- Annual performance reviews occur in December, with salary changes effective February 1.
- Mid-year adjustments may be made for promotions or significant role changes.
- Market adjustments are reviewed annually by the Compensation team.

## Merit Increases

- Standard merit increase range: 2–6% based on performance rating.
- Exceptional performers (top 10%) may receive up to 10%.
- Employees on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) are not eligible for merit increases.

## Bonuses

- Annual bonus target is 10% of base salary for individual contributors, 15% for managers.
- Bonuses are paid in March and are based on company performance and individual rating.
- Employees must be active on the payout date to receive a bonus.

## Requesting a Raise

Employees may submit a compensation review request outside the annual cycle by discussing with their manager and submitting a formal request to HR with supporting justification (e.g., market data, expanded responsibilities). These requests are reviewed quarterly.

## Pay Equity

The company conducts annual pay equity analyses. Employees who believe there is a pay discrepancy based on gender, race, or other protected characteristics should contact HR confidentially.

## Salary Transparency

Salary bands for each role are available in the HR portal. Employees are encouraged to discuss compensation with HR if they have questions about their band placement.
